Why Lumina

Lumina Terra is the culmination of Dr. Gandhi’s extensive research & experience, embodying her vision of creating Lucknow’s most iconic school. It offers a superb educational experience with world-class infrastructure & numerous facilities. At Sky High, we are committed to providing holistic education that includes academic excellence, the development of the whole person, & the pursuit of excellence in all things.

Our name reflects our aspiration to empower students to reach for the skies & realize their full potential. We believe in cultivating a learning environment where every individual can soar to new heights of achievement & fulfillment. We remain steadfast in our mission to provide a well-rounded education that prepares students for success in a rapidly evolving world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment where students can develop 21st Century Skills & Traits, enabling them to become confident, compassionate, & capable leaders who contribute positively to society, reflecting the belief that education should serve humanity & foster the development of noble qualities.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every student at Sky High Futures School is equipped with the skills, traits, & knowledge needed to thrive in an interconnected world, making meaningful contributions to their communities & beyond, embodying the vision of unity & service.

Goal of Education

At Sky High, we recognize that education extends beyond academic achievement. Our goal is to empower students with a comprehensive skill set & a strong moral compass, preparing them to navigate life's challenges with confidence & resilience. Through a curriculum that emphasizes life skills, we aim to cultivate well-rounded individuals who are equipped to excel in all aspects of life, aligning with the teaching that education should develop capacities for service & contribute to social progress.
