The World’s Most
Outstanding Sustainable School

At Lumina Terra, we redefine education as a transformative journey where sustainability, innovation, creativity, & leadership converge. Our mission is to prepare students to become global leaders and changemakers of tomorrow. Derived from Latin, Terra means Earth or land, symbolizing sustainability and a profound connection to the natural world. Nova Terra represents a “New Earth,” blending environmental stewardship with educational innovation to inspire meaningful transformation.
