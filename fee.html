Key Notes for Parents ‐ Lumina Terra School
  • The Security Deposit of 45,000 is fully refundable at the time of withdrawal, subject to clearance of all dues and return of school property in good condition.
  • The 90,000 is a one-time, non-refundable admission fee, separate from the annual tuition and transportation.
  • This investment supports a safe, technologically advanced, and personalized learning environment, with infrastructure, resources, and support systems designed to nurture holistic development.
Quarterly Fee Payment Schedule
  • Quarterly fees of 90000 are due on the 1st of the beginning month of each quarter.
  • A grace period until the 10th of the same month will be provided to complete the payment without penalty.
