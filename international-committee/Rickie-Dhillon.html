Rickie Dhillon

Superintendent/CEO of Kepler Neighborhood School in Fresno, California

Rickie Dhillon is a visionary leader transforming financially distressed districts into thriving economic hubs. With a proven track record of driving growth, creating jobs, and improving quality of life, Rickie's expertise has made a lasting impact on underserved communities.

>Her personal journey, from witnessing her hometown struggle with economic decline to becoming a catalyst for change, fuels her passion for revitalization. Rickie shares her innovative approach to economic transformation, inspiring leaders and communities to reimagine their own potential.
