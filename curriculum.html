our curriculum

At Lumina Terra, your child’s journey is designed around distinct stages of growth, each with a unique identity, learning approach, and set of experiences. Our curriculum is inspired by the world’s most respected education systems, guided by an international committee, and shaped by evidence-backed, future-focused practices.

Tender Growth, New Beginnings

  • Play-based numeracy and literacy, early writing, and language play
  • Sensory discovery, nature walks, gardening, practical life skills
  • International language exposure and music
  • Creative movement, art, and storytelling
  • Social-emotional foundations: empathy, gratitude, sharing, circle time

Expanding Roots, Building Foundations

  • Daily math, language, and science through inquiry and play
  • Global languages, arts, sports, teamwork
  • Early coding, STEAM, and design thinking
  • Outdoor exploration, field trips, and eco-awareness
  • Life skills: baking, sewing, organizing, financial sense
  • Community engagement and celebration of learning
Emergence of vibrant curiosity-light begins to play, explore, and connect

Leadership Seeds, ideas about to blossom

  • Coding, robotics, digital portfolios, and creative writing
  • Debate, Model UN, public speaking, and leadership roles

  • Adventure and service: volunteering, community projects
  • Maker spaces: 3D printing, film-making, design challenges

Discovery, Energy, and Self-Expression
